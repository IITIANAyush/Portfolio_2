Who I Am

I'm Ayush Bhaskar, a B.Tech student in Aerospace Engineering at IIT Bombay. I build things that move, sense, and think — from autonomous drones and robotic navigators to ML-powered vision systems and LLM-driven swarm controllers.

My work spans the full stack: Euler–Lagrange dynamics modelling, ROS2 navigation stacks, computer vision pipelines, and consumer-grade hardware prototyping. I'm equally at home deriving equations on paper and soldering circuits on a bench.
