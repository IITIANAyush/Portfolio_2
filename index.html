01 // Who I am

About Me

I'm a 3rd-year B.Tech. Aerospace Engineering student at IIT Bombay, building at the intersection of autonomous systems, robotics, and flight mechanics. My work ranges from implementing Kalman filters and guidance laws to physically assembling and flying RC aircraft and rover platforms.

On the software side I work in Python, C/C++, MATLAB, and ROS 2 — writing planners, controllers, and simulation models. On the hardware side I design in Fusion 360, cut acrylic, 3D print, solder, and tune PIDs on real vehicles.

I served as Technical Convener of the Aeromodelling Club, IIT Bombay — organising a 340-participant competition, running iNav workshops, and mentoring student teams through UAV expos and RC airshows.
